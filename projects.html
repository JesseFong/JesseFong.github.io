Game Engine

My personal game engine. Used as a conglomeration of various game technology that I have worked on. A few a which include:
-Separated deferred PBR renderer with OpenGL and DirectX backends
-Mesh skinning animation system
-Chunk based world streaming
-P2P Rollback Netcode with GGPO
-An in game entity and level editor

The art comes from KayKit over at their Itch.io page.
